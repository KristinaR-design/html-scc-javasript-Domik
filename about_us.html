ABOUT

NEAR THE RIVER

Your cozy corner in the heart of the mountains!

Our hotel is located next to a picturesque river, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes and the purest mountain air. Here nature intertwines with comfort, creating an ideal place to relax from the bustle of the city.
We offer comfortable rooms, an atmosphere of coziness and hospitality, as well as a variety of activities for every taste - from hiking and fishing to relaxing evenings by the fireplace. At NEAR THE RIVER, every detail is thought out to make your holiday unforgettable.
Come for inspiration, silence and harmony - you are always welcome here!


Our services

We offer accommodation that is suitable for any type of holiday. For a romantic getaway or a holiday for two, the cozy A small house for two is ideal. Families and small groups can choose the spacious Large house for 4 people, and for larger groups, the Large house for 6 people is perfect. If you want to enjoy privacy and comfort, rent the entire plot for 8 people, where it will be just you and your loved ones.
To make your holiday even more enjoyable, we offer a real Russian bathhouse - a place where you can relax and recuperate after a busy day in the mountains. Your comfort and unforgettable impressions are our priority!
