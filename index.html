Cozy getaway in nature — come and immerse yourself in the peaceful silence!


Imagine a cozy place nestled in the heart of the mountains, where time slows down, and nature surrounds you at every turn. Our cabins, located right by the river, are perfect for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse themselves in the tranquility and beauty of the great outdoors. Here, you’ll find everything you need for complete relaxation: a hot steam bath to unwind and warm up after a day of exploring, a grill for preparing delicious meals in the fresh air, and, of course, the cabins themselves, designed for maximum comfort and coziness.

Every day here begins with the enchanting sounds of birdsong and crisp mountain air, and in the evening, you can gaze at the stars while sitting by the grill or relaxing by the river. This place is ideal for family getaways, romantic weekends, or gatherings with friends. No matter the season, our little haven in nature will give you unforgettable memories, energy, and inner peace.
